NEED HELP AT BORDER/AIRPORT?

  • Remember, you have rights. Refuse to relinquish them.
  • Demand a hearing from a judge.
  • Demand to speak to an attorney.
  • Green card holders: Refuse any demands to give up your green card AND refuse to sign Form I-407
Avoid traveling abroad if you are currently within US borders — you may not be able to renter the country.

  • Airbnb is coordinating free housing for refugees and others turned away at border
  • Some lawyers recommending rerouting flights back to the US through Boston (the city with the most expansive court order)

Muslim Ban Quick Reference

Full text

Countries included: Iraq, Syria, Iran, Libya, Somalia, Sudan, and Yemen.

Quick status update: Federal court has ordered federal government to allow entrance of refugees and visa holders. Some Customs Border Patrol refuse to allow entry despite order (JFK, NY, Dulles, DC). CBP also reportedly refusing to allow detainees legal council in some airports (Dulles, Washington DC), as well as access to medication and other personal belongings (Dulles, Washington DC). Department of Homeland Security is also refusing to accept court ruling (Press release).
