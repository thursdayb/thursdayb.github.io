Muslim Ban Quick Reference

Full text

Countries included: Iraq, Syria, Iran, Libya, Somalia, Sudan, and Yemen.

Quick status update: Federal court has ordered federal government to allow entrance of refugees and visa holders. Some Customs Border Patrol refuse to allow entry despite order (JFK, NY, Dulles, DC). CBP also reportedly refusing to allow detainees legal council in some airports (Dulles, Washington DC), as well as access to medication and other personal belongings (). Department of Homeland Security is also refusing to accept court ruling (Press release).

Legal Actions

  • Nationally: United States District Court, Eastern District of New York ruled that until a hearing can be held, individuals with approved refugee applications and holders of visas already granted are legally authorized to enter US (Full text). (Analysis of the stay's impact)
  • Washington State: Governor Inslee said on CNN that Washington State is planning a challenge to the ban.
  • Washington State: Washington State has a federal stay on airport detentions.
